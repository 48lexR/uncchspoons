How this will work...

When the time is right, you will be given a target, which will display on this website. Your goal is to "kill" your target with a spoon, which will also have their name on it.

Please read the following rules in order to properly play:

  1. You must "kill" a person by tapping your spoon on their shoulder. You must submit your kills within 24 hours of having killed your target.
  2. You are safe if you are holding your spoon to your nose. Your hand must be touching your spoon (gloves allowed, but your hand must be in the glove.)
  3. Below is a list of safe spaces:
    • In class
    • Five minutes before, and five minutes after a class change
    • At a club event or meeting
    • Where "killing" another person could potentially harm either the killer or the target.
  4. Failure to abide by these rules will result in automatic expulsion from the game and disqualification from competing in the future.
  5. The winner of this years' competition will be in charge of overseeing the game next year. The winner is the last standing competitor.
